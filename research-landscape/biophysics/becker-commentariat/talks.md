# Becker — recorded talks (commentariat / landscape layer, NOT canon)

Secondary sources. Do not cite in canon-tier material without tracing back
to the underlying paper.

## Becker himself

- **Bioelectromagnetics Society (BEMS) annual meetings**, multiple
  invited talks, 1979–1992. Abstracts in *Bioelectromagnetics* journal.
- **1985 New York State Powerlines Project** — presentation of the
  project's final-report findings (Becker was a principal investigator).
- **Project Sanguine / Seafarer hearings**, U.S. Navy ELF-communications
  program, mid-1970s. Transcripts in *Congressional Record*.
- **National Institutes of Health** invited seminar, Bethesda, 1976, on
  current-of-injury and limb regeneration.

## Others speaking on Becker's legacy

- **Michael Levin**, "The Computational Boundary of a 'Self'" — Allen
  Institute / Santa Fe Institute talks 2019–2023. Names Becker as the
  experimental precursor to the modern bioelectric-morphogenesis program.
- **James Oschman**, keynote at ISSSEEM (International Society for the
  Study of Subtle Energies and Energy Medicine), multiple years.
- **Gerald Pollack**, "The Fourth Phase of Water" (University of
  Washington lecture series, 2013–) — references Becker's
  semiconductor-bone results.
- **Beverly Rubik**, talks on biofield science (various, 2000s–2020s).

## Archival note

robertobecker.net hosts an index of media appearances at
/later-life-public-testimony and /regeneration-articles. The estate has not,

material is reconstructed from TV network archives (CBS, PBS) and
