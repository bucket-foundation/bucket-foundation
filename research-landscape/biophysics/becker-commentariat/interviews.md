# Becker — interviews (commentariat / landscape layer, NOT canon)

Secondary material. Useful for provenance and public-record quotes; not a
primary source. Canon-tier claims must cite the underlying paper or book,
not the interview.

## Becker himself

- **1965 American Chemical Society interview** — audio/transcript held at
  robertobecker.net/1965-acs-interview. Early statement of the DC-control
  hypothesis.
- **1977 CBS *60 Minutes* segment** on bone regeneration and EMF. Indexed at
  robertobecker.net/60-minutes.
- **1985 Congressional testimony** (House Subcommittee on Water and Power
  Resources; NY State Powerlines Project hearings). Index:
  robertobecker.net/public-health-congressional-testimony.
- **Interview with Julian Gresser**, *Piezo Communications*, 1997
  (environmental-EMF policy).
- **Interview in *Explore!* magazine** vol. 7, 1996 — late-career summary
  of the regeneration program.

## Others on Becker

- **Andrew Marino**, multiple recorded lectures at LSU Medical School and
  the Bioelectromagnetics Society annual meetings (1990s–2000s).
- **Michael Levin** — interviews on Sean Carroll's *Mindscape* (2020),
  Lex Fridman (#313, 2022), and Theories of Everything (2023) name Becker
  as the bridge figure between pre-molecular bioelectric biology and the
  modern voltage-reporter / optogenetic era.
- **Jerry Tennant**, *Healing Is Voltage* (2010, self-published) — cites
  Becker heavily; treat as popularization, not canon.
