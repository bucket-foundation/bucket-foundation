# Radiosynthesis ↔ Photosynthesis Thread (seed)

**Bead**: `bkt-research-08`. Seed entry — to be expanded into a full dossier alongside `becker/` after DOI pipeline lands.

## Thesis

Energy capture from electromagnetic radiation is continuous across deep time: from early photosynthetic pathways (~3.5 Gya cyanobacteria; canonical with Great Oxygenation Event ~2.4 Gya) through to observed **radiosynthesis** — melanized fungi growing toward ionizing-radiation sources at Chernobyl. Both are light-driven energy-capture mechanisms operating on different parts of the EM spectrum, converging on conserved molecular machinery (porphyrin/melanin chemistries).

This is a *foundation-tier* claim when stated as mechanism, and only mechanism. Clinical / longevity extrapolations are downstream (`05-biophysics/sub-outcomes/longevity/`) and do not enter canon at this level.
