# Books by Robert O. Becker

Citation-only. No PDFs stored. Acquire via library, WorldCat, or purchase.

## 1. *Electromagnetism and Life*

- Authors: Robert O. Becker and Andrew A. Marino
- Publisher: SUNY Press, Albany, NY
- Year: 1982
- ISBN: 978-0-87395-560-0 (pbk)
- OCLC: 7875629
- WorldCat: https://www.worldcat.org/oclc/7875629
- OpenLibrary: https://openlibrary.org/search?q=electromagnetism+and+life+becker+marino
- Note: Technical monograph. The most detailed primary exposition of the
  DC-control-system hypothesis and the EMF-biology evidence base as of the
  early 1980s. Out of print; full text placed online by the authors —
  Marino's site hosts a legal open edition at
  https://ortho.lsuhsc.edu/Faculty/Marino/EL/ELTOC.html

## 2. *The Body Electric: Electromagnetism and the Foundation of Life*

- Authors: Robert O. Becker and Gary Selden
- Publisher: William Morrow, New York
- Year: 1985
- ISBN: 978-0-688-06971-9 (hc); 978-0-688-06971-0; 978-0-688-06971-9
  (Quill pbk 0-688-06971-1)
- OCLC: 11622782
- WorldCat: https://www.worldcat.org/oclc/11622782
- OpenLibrary: https://openlibrary.org/works/OL1964818W/The_body_electric
- Note: Canonical popular account. Primary narrative source for the
  salamander regeneration experiments, Project Sanguine controversy, and
  the semiconductor-bone thesis.

Electropollution*

- Author: Robert O. Becker
- Publisher: Jeremy P. Tarcher / Putnam, Los Angeles
- Year: 1990
- ISBN: 978-0-87477-536-4 (hc); 978-0-87477-609-5 (pbk, 1991)
- OCLC: 21970326
- WorldCat: https://www.worldcat.org/oclc/21970326
- OpenLibrary: https://openlibrary.org/works/OL3939833W/Cross_currents
- Note: Updates *The Body Electric* with the 1985–1990 EMF-exposure
